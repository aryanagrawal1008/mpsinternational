Brief History of School

MPS International, a Co-educational CBSE Affiliated English Medium School, located at Bhabha Marg, Tilak Nagar, Jaipur, founded in 2014 with affiliation no: 1730679, is modern and attractive in both layout and construction. The school is run under the guidance of The Education Committee of The Maheshwari Samaj that has a network of ten educational institutions in Jaipur. With a strength of approximately two thousand students, around seventy five well qualified teachers, skilful physical training and co-curricular activity coaches, a proficient counselor the aim of the school is the all round formation of the students to make them good human beings and worthy citizens of the country that is rich in diverse cultures, religions, languages and customs. The school strives to provide a high quality education within a safe and caring environment. There is a strong focus on academics and co-curricular activities. A wide range of extra-curricular opportunities are provided including the experience of organizing and participating in inter-school tournaments & competitions.
